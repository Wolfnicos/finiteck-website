Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an expense?

Tap the "+" button on the Expenses tab to add a new recurring expense. Enter the name, amount, category, and frequency.

How does the AI advisor work?

Our AI analyzes your expenses and compares them with market data to find personalized savings opportunities. It provides actionable suggestions with specific tips on how to reduce costs.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it. We don't have access to your financial information. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Profile > Subscription and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase the subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone or iPad to manage or cancel your Finiteck subscription.

How do I export my data?

Go to Profile > Data > Export Data. This will generate a file with all your expenses and savings information.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Profile > Data > Delete All Data. This will permanently remove all your expenses, suggestions, and profile data from your device.

The app is in the wrong language

Go to Profile > Settings and select your preferred language from the Language picker. The app supports English, French, and Romanian.
