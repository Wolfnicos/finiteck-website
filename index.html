Frequently Asked Questions

What is Finiteck and how does it work?

Finiteck is a personal money-saving assistant for iOS. You add your recurring expenses (subscriptions, utilities, services), and the AI analyzes your spending to find personalized savings opportunities. All data stays on your device — no bank connections needed.

Is my financial data safe with Finiteck?

Yes. Finiteck stores all data locally on your iPhone using Apple's encryption. We never upload your information to any server. There are zero analytics, zero third-party SDKs, and zero ads. We have no access to your financial data. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

How much does Finiteck cost?

Finiteck offers a 7-day free trial with no payment required upfront. After the trial, you can choose a monthly plan at €3.99/month or an annual plan at €39.99/year (€3.33/month). You can cancel anytime through your App Store settings.

Does Finiteck require bank connections?

No. Finiteck never asks for your bank credentials. You manually add the expenses you want to track. This approach ensures maximum privacy — your financial information never leaves your device.

What languages does Finiteck support?

Finiteck currently supports English, French, and Romanian. You can change the language at any time from Profile > Settings in the app.
